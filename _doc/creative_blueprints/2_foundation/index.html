The Foundation

Why onboarding is where revenue leaks

The Problem

New signups quietly stall mid-onboarding and never reach first value. With no single place to see who's stuck and no reliable way to nudge them, those customers churn before they ever become real, paying users — and the loss stays invisible until the revenue doesn't show up.

The Solution

One focused workspace where the team sees every customer's onboarding status at a glance and triggers email reminders to move stalled customers forward — turning onboarding from a forgettable manual chore into a tracked, repeatable process.

Positioned as a lightweight onboarding tracker that protects new-customer activation revenue — not a heavyweight CRM.
